Garlic Parmesan Garden Rotini

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 box of garden rotini
  • 3/4 can of olives
  • 1 tomato
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • Parmesan
  • Oregano
  • Basil
Estimated cost: $4
Directions:

Bring a pot of water to boil and add garden rotini. Pour olive oil into a separate pan over medium heat and add olives and chopped garlic and tomato. When the rotini is soft, drain and pour over the olive and tomato mixutre. Stir in parmesan and season with basil and oregano.
