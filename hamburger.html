Hamburger

Ingredients:
  • 1 hamburger patty
  • 1/2 of a tomato
  • 3 pickle slices
  • Onion
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Salt
  • 1 ciabatta roll
Estimated cost: $3
Directions:

Cook the hamburger patty in a pan, flipping every few minutes and salting each side of the patty once. Slice the tomato and cut some thin slices from an onion. Place the tomatoes, pickles and onion slices on the bottom half of a ciabatta roll, and put a small amount of ketchup and mustard on the top half. Once the patty is fully cooked, place it over the vegetables and place the top half of the roll on top.
