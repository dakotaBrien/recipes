Tuna Sandwich

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 can of tuna
  • 1/2 tomato
  • 5 black olives
  • 3 pickle slices
  • Onion
  • Mayo
  • Mustard
  • Salt
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Olive oil
  • 1 ciabatta roll
Estimated cost: $3
Directions:

Slice the tomato and set it to the side. Slice the olives into 3rds and add them to a bowl. Open and drain the can of tuna and add half of it to the bowl along with salt, 1/4 cup of mayo, 2tbsp of mustard and red pepper flakes. Mix well and scoop onto the bottom half of the ciabatta roll. Top with tomato slices, pickles, and thin slices of onions. Lightly drizzle a small amount of olive oil over the veggies before replacing the top half of the ciabatta roll.
