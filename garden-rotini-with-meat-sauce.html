Garden Rotini with Meat Suace

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 box of garden rotini
  • 1/2 lbs of ground pork
  • 1 jar of marinara
  • Salt
  • Oregano
  • Basil
Estimated cost: $5
Directions:

Bring a pot of water to boil and add garden rotini. Add ground pork to a separate pan over medium heat and lightly salt. When the rotini is soft, drain and pour over the meat. Stir in marinara and season with basil and oregano.
