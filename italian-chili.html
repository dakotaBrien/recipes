Italian Chili

Ingredients:
  • 2 cans of cannellini beans
  • 1/2lbs of ground pork
  • 1/2 onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tomato
  • 8oz can of tomato sauce
  • Salt
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Oregano
  • Basil
Estimated cost: $5
Directions:

Pour olive oil into a pan over medium heat. Chop the onion and garlic and add them to the pan. Cook until the onions are soft, then add the ground pork. Let the pork begin to brown before adding the chopped tomato, cannellini beans and tomato sauce. Season with salt, red pepper flakes, basil and oregano. Keep on medium heat until fully cooked.
