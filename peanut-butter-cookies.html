Peanut Butter Cookies

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup of peanut butter
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 egg
Estimated cost: $2
Directions:

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Combine ingredients into a mixing bowl and mix well. Place parchment paper on a baking tray and use a spoon to place scoops of cookie mixture spaced evenly across the tray. Bake for 9 minutes and let cool.
