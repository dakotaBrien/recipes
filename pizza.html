Pizza

Ingredients:
  • 1 bag of Great Value crust mixture
  • 1/2 bag of pepperoni
  • 8oz can of tomato sauce
  • Shredded cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon Juice
  • Oregano
  • Basil
Estimated cost: $6
Directions:

Preheat the oven to 475 degrees. Pour crust mixture and a dash of olive oil into a mixing bowl and mix with hot water until the mixture reaches a doughy consistency. Place parchment paper on a pizza tray, press the dough evenly and let sit for 5 minutes. In a separate mixing bowl, mix together tomato sauce, a dash of lemon juice, oregano and basil. Bake the crust for 5 minutes before topping with pizza sauce, cheese and pepperoni. Bake for an additional 12 minutes and let cool before cutting.
